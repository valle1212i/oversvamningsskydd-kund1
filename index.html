Flood protection FAQs

Answers to common flood protection questions.

How do flood barriers function?

Flood barriers block or redirect water from entry points like doors and driveways, helping keep buildings dry during heavy rain or rising water.

Does flood protection affect insurance?

Installing flood protection can reduce risk, which may qualify your property for lower insurance premiums with many providers.

What upkeep do systems need?

Inspect and clean flood protection systems regularly. Check seals, pumps, and barriers, especially before wet seasons.

Are basements protected by these systems?

Specialized barriers and pumps can help keep basements dry. Proper installation and maintenance are essential for effective protection.
